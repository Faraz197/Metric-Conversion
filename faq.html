To enter numbers in scientific notation, write them as a decimal number (mantissa) between 1 and 10, followed by "× 10" raised to the appropriate power. For example, 4500 is written as 4.5 × 1 0 3 4.5×10 3 , and 0.00012 is written as 1.2 × 1 0 − 4 1.2×10 −4 .

A knot is a speed unit. A nautical mile is a distance unit.

Rainfall refers to the amount of water that falls as precipitation, typically measured in inches or millimeters. To convert from inches to millimeters, multiply by 25.4; to convert from millimeters to inches, divide by 25.4.

The long ton (UK ton) is 2,240 pounds (1,016 kg), the short ton (US ton) is 2,000 pounds (907.18 kg), and the metric ton (tonne) is 2,204.62 pounds (1,000 kg).

Celsius and Fahrenheit are the same at -40°.
